About Me

Kidding, again, I actually took it because I’ve always taken loads of STEM classes my entire life because that’s what I’m good at and I told my parents that I’m “probably gonna major in comp sci or something like that,” but in reality I have no intention of going into any STEM-related fields because I realized that STEM jobs sucks big booty cheeks so instead I want to explore media production-related careers, but that’s only something I figured out about myself like within the last year, so I haven’t gotten around to telling my parents so I’m kind of just slowly, smoothly easing myself into being a Com Arts major.

As for my biggest fears: my biggest fear in life is that I will not pass Communication Arts 155. So if the person reading this happens to be my TA, please give me a good grade Samantha. I’m begging you. I need this. Please.
